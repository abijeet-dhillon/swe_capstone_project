The project aims to provide a privacy-focused tool for creators (students or early professionals) to scan their machines for digital work artifacts (like code, documents, and media) and generate portfolio highlights. Administrators configure scanning policies, while Reviewers access only the processed summaries, not raw files. The solution includes a local-first architecture with three components:  
- **Scanner:** Identifies files in user-approved folders.
